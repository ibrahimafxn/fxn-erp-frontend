# Business guide

## Goals

- Control stock and depots in near real time.
- Track resources (materials, consumables, vehicles).
- Manage interventions, reservations, orders, and receipts.
- Monitor performance through dashboards and reports.

## Target profiles

- Depot management
- Admin / Executive
- Technician (based on role)

## Access and navigation

- Sign in via the `login` page.
- The workspace depends on your role:
  - Depot: stock and logistics operations.
  - Technician: field activity and consultations.
  - Admin: global supervision and administration.

## Daily operations (examples)

- Review dashboards and stock alerts.
- Create or review an order.
- Follow reservations and receipts.
- Use history to track movements.
- Maintain records for materials, consumables, and vehicles.

## Best practices

- Check stock alerts before any resource release.
- Keep resource data complete for traceability.
- Use history logs to validate discrepancies.
