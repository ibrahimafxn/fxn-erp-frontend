# Modules and screens

## Administration (Admin / Executive role)

- Dashboard: global operations view.
- Onboarding: initial setup and guidance.
- Depots: create, list, detail, edit.
- Users: create, detail, edit.
- Security: user access management.
- History: actions and movements log.
- Reservations: request tracking and allocation.
- Receipts: document tracking and validation.
- Orders: create, track, detail.
- Stock alerts: critical thresholds and actions.
- Interventions: dashboard, import, technician detail.
- BPU: types and prestations.
- Revenue: financial indicators (if enabled).
- Resources > Materials: list, detail, create, edit.
- Resources > Consumables: list, detail, create, edit.
- Resources > Vehicles: list, detail, create, edit, breakdowns.
- HR: HR records (if enabled).
- Technicians: activity and interventions.

## Depot (Depot management role)

- Depot dashboard.
- Allocation: resource attribution.
- Resources: view materials, consumables, vehicles.
- History, reservations, receipts, stock alerts.

## Technician

- Technician dashboard.
- Resources: view materials, consumables, vehicles.
- Reports: production and review.
- Revenue: summary view (if enabled).
- History: activity tracking.
