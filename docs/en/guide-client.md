# Client guide

## Goals

- Understand the functional scope of FXN ERP.
- Identify the tracked KPIs for management reporting.
- Clarify roles and responsibilities.

## Functional scope

- Depot and stock management.
- Resources: materials, consumables, vehicles.
- Interventions and field activity tracking.
- Orders, reservations, and receipts.
- Performance tracking (dashboards, revenue if enabled).

## Common KPIs (depending on configuration)

- Volumes per depot and period.
- Consumption and stock-out rates.
- Technician activity (interventions, reports).
- Revenue or receipts evolution.

## Governance and access

- Roles: Executive, Admin, Depot management, Technician.
- Roles determine available screens and permissions.

## Control points

- Movement history and traceability.
- Stock alert monitoring.
- Reservation and receipt tracking.
